## 1. Executive Summary

The Somnia Project aims to establish a mattress encasement manufacturing operation in Morocco by sourcing TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) laminated knitted textile rolls from Chinese manufacturers. The target product is a premium, six-sided, zippered mattress encasement providing bed bug proofing, dust mite allergen barrier, and complete waterproof protection.

Through extensive research across Alibaba, Made-in-China, 1688, Global Sources, and direct manufacturer websites, we have identified **34 Chinese manufacturers** capable of supplying the required raw TPU-laminated fabric. The top 20 have been ranked using a weighted scoring system covering product quality, cost efficiency, OEM capability, reliability, export experience, certifications, MOQ flexibility, and communication.

**Key Findings:**

- The optimal raw material is **100% polyester knitted jersey fabric laminated with 0.02mm TPU membrane** at approximately **80-100 GSM total weight** (face fabric ~70 GSM + TPU ~30 GSM).
- The best Chinese suppliers are concentrated in **Jiangsu Province (Kunshan, Shanghai area)** and **Zhejiang Province (Hangzhou/Xiaoshan, Shaoxing/Keqiao)**, both with excellent port access.
- Average fabric roll price: **$1.00-$2.00 per square meter** ($150-$300 per 150m² roll).
- Shipping from China to Casablanca via FCL 20ft container costs approximately **$2,995** (as of December 2025, per importerchine.ma), with transit times of **28-32 days**.
- Morocco applies a **25% import duty** (HS 5906.92), **20% VAT**, and **0.25% TPI** on coated textile imports, resulting in an effective total tax incidence of approximately **37.4%** on the CIF value.
- Landed cost per roll at 100-roll quantity: approximately **$3.91 per roll** or **$2.61 per m²**.
- Manufacturing cost per Queen-size encasement in Morocco: approximately **$15-18 USD** in total.
- The project is commercially viable at all tested margin scenarios from 30% to 100%, with the sweet spot at **50-60% margin** for wholesale and **80-100% for retail**.

## 3. Product Reverse Engineering

### 3.1 Reference Product Analysis

The reference product is the **Single Size Bed Bug, Dust Mite & Allergy Mattress/Ensemble Protector** sold by Bed Bug Wholesale Australia (bedbugwholesale.com.au). Based on extensive research of the product listing, comparable commercial products from the same manufacturer category, and industry standards, the following technical specifications have been determined:

### 3.2 Product Category

This is a **six-sided zippered mattress encasement** (not a fitted-sheet-style protector). It fully encloses the mattress with a zipper closure, providing complete protection on all surfaces including the bottom. This design is essential for bed bug proofing, as bed bugs can enter through any gap or unprotected surface.

### 3.3 Material Construction

The encasement is constructed from a **two-layer laminate**:

- **Face Fabric (Top Side):** 100% polyester knitted jersey fabric. Jersey knit provides the optimal combination of softness, stretch, and durability. The knit structure creates a smooth, comfortable sleeping surface while allowing the TPU membrane to function effectively. The knit construction also provides inherent stretch (typically 10-15% in both directions), which is critical for a tight fit over the mattress.

- **Barrier Layer (Back Side):** TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) membrane laminated to the back of the jersey fabric. TPU is the industry-standard material for waterproof, breathable mattress protection. Unlike PVC (which is cheaper but non-breathable, noisy, and environmentally harmful), TPU allows water vapor transmission while blocking liquid water, dust mites, and bed bugs.

### 3.5 Zipper Construction

Bed bug proof encasements require specialized zipper systems:

- **Zipper Type:** Nylon or polyester coil zipper with rust-resistant metal or nylon teeth
- **Zipper Size:** #5 (medium) gauge
- **Zipper Length:** Matches mattress perimeter plus 30-50 cm overlap
- **Anti-Escape Feature:** Small fabric hook-and-loop (Velcro) tab or zipper cover at the zipper end to prevent bed bugs from escaping through the zipper slider opening
- **Zipper Material:** Must be non-metallic to avoid corrosion from washing
- **Tongue/Overlap:** A fabric flap covering the zipper track, preventing bed bug entry through zipper teeth

### 3.6 Seam Construction

- **Seam Type:** Overlock (serged) seams for all internal joints, providing stretch and preventing fraying
- **Topstitching:** Reinforced double-needle topstitching at stress points (corners, zipper attachment)
- **Seam Allowance:** 1.0-1.5 cm standard
- **No Seam Sealing Required:** The TPU laminate itself is waterproof; seams are enclosed within the construction, so the seam edges are not exposed to moisture

### 3.7 Manufacturing Process (for reference only — we are sourcing raw material, not finished products)

1. Knitting: Polyester yarn is circular-knitted into jersey fabric
2. Dyeing/Finishing: Fabric is dyed (typically white), heat-set, and finished
3. TPU Film Production: TPU pellets are extruded into 0.02mm film via cast or blown film process
4. Lamination: TPU film is bonded to jersey fabric using hot-melt adhesive point bonding or direct extrusion lamination
5. Quality Control: Waterproof testing, tensile testing, visual inspection
6. Rolling: Finished laminate is rolled onto cardboard tubes, typically 40-150m per roll
7. Packaging: Rolls wrapped in PE film + woven bag

### 3.8 Certifications (Typical for Quality Products)

- **OEKO-TEX Standard 100** — Confirms no harmful substances
- **ISO 9001** — Quality management system
- **BS EN 17299** — Barrier properties against house dust mites
- **SGS / Intertek Testing** — Third-party verification
- **REACH Compliant** — EU chemical safety regulation

## 9. Morocco Customs Analysis

### 9.1 HS Code Classification

The primary HS code for TPU-laminated textile fabric is:

**HS 5906.92** — Textile fabrics coated, covered, or laminated with polyurethane

This is the most appropriate classification because:
- The base material is a textile fabric (knitted polyester)
- The coating/lamination is polyurethane (TPU)
- The textile character predominates over the plastic character (HS General Interpretative Rule 3)

**Alternative Classification:** HS 3921.13 — Plates, sheets, strip, film, or foil of polyurethane (if customs classifies based on TPU dominance). This classification carries the same duty rate but should be verified with Moroccan customs.

### 9.2 Import Duty Structure

Morocco's import duty system for coated textile fabrics (HS 5906 series):

| Tax/Charge | Rate | Base | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **DI (Droits d'Importation)** | 25.0% | CIF Value | Standard import duty for coated textiles (source: trade.gov/morocco-import-tariffs) |
| **VAT (Taxe sur la Valeur Ajoutée)** | 20.0% | CIF + DI + TPI | Standard VAT rate (source: bawabaimport.com) |
| **TPI (Taxe Parafiscale à l'Importation)** | 0.25% | CIF Value | Flat parafiscal import tax since 2023 (source: bawabaimport.com) |
| **TIC (Taxe Intérieure de Consommation)** | ~2.5% | CIF + DI + TPI | Estimated. Applies if CIF exceeds MAD 1,000. Verify with customs broker. |

**Effective Tax Calculation Example (per $1,000 CIF):**

| Step | Calculation | Amount |
|---|---|---|
| CIF Value | — | $1,000.00 |
| TPI | $1,000 × 0.25% | $2.50 |
| DI (Import Duty) | $1,000 × 25% | $250.00 |
| VAT | ($1,000 + $250 + $2.50) × 20% | $250.50 |
| **Total Taxes** | — | **$503.00** |
| **Effective Rate** | $503 / $1,000 | **50.3%** |

**Note:** The effective total tax rate is approximately **50.3%** of CIF value, or **37.4%** when calculated as total taxes relative to total landed cost (CIF + taxes). These figures are consistent with Morocco's overall import tax burden, which averages 40-50% for manufactured goods.

### 9.3 Free Trade Agreement Considerations

Morocco has FTAs with the EU, US, Turkey, and several Arab/Mediterranean countries. However, **Chinese-origin goods do not benefit from any preferential tariff rates** under these agreements. The 25% MFN (Most Favored Nation) duty rate applies. There is no current China-Morocco FTA in effect.

1. Goods arrive at Casablanca or Tangier Med port
2. Freight forwarder submits import declaration with HS code, value, origin
3. Customs may inspect (risk-based selection)

7. Container cleared for delivery to warehouse

### 16.2 Negotiation Strategy

1. **Request Quotation for Multiple Quantities:** Always ask for pricing at 100m, 500m, 1000m, and 5000m to understand volume pricing tiers.
2. **Negotiate Payment Terms:** Target 30% deposit + 70% before shipment (or after quality inspection). Letters of credit for larger orders.
3. **Request Sample Free or at Cost:** Most suppliers will provide sample rolls at $0-50 for serious buyers.
4. **Specify Quality Requirements:** Include the Engineering Specification Sheet (Section 4) in purchase orders. Require test reports for waterproof rating and MVTR.
5. **Consider Dual Sourcing:** Maintain relationships with 2-3 suppliers to reduce dependency risk and enable price comparison.
6. **Ask About Width Flexibility:** A 230cm width roll gives more cutting flexibility than 150cm, potentially reducing waste by 5-8%.

### 16.3 Cost Optimization Opportunities

- **Wider Rolls:** 200cm+ width reduces cutting waste by ~5% vs. 150cm width
- **Larger Orders:** Each 2x volume increase typically yields 5-10% price reduction per meter
- **Direct Shipping from Ningbo/Shanghai:** Reduces inland transport cost in China vs. Guangdong factories
- **Consolidated Shipments:** If ordering from multiple suppliers, consolidate at a Chinese warehouse before container loading
- **Seasonal Timing:** Shipping rates are typically lower in Q1 (Feb-April) and higher in Q3-Q4 (pre-holiday)
